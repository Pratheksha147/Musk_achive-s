

# Elon Musk — Achievements Timeline Page

A clean, single-page website showcasing Elon Musk's key achievements throughout his journey.

## Layout & Sections

1. **Hero Section** — A bold header with Elon Musk's name, a short tagline ("Innovator. Entrepreneur. Visionary."), and a subtle background gradient.

2. **Bio Summary** — A brief 2-3 sentence introduction about who Elon Musk is.

3. **Achievements Timeline** — A vertical timeline highlighting major milestones:
   - **1995** — Zip2 (first startup)
   - **1999** — X.com / PayPal
   - **2002** — SpaceX founded
   - **2004** — Joined Tesla as Chairman & later CEO
   - **2006** — SolarCity co-founded
   - **2016** — Neuralink & The Boring Company
   - **2020** — SpaceX Crew Dragon (first private crewed mission to ISS)
   - **2022** — Twitter/X acquisition
   - **2024** — Starship milestones

4. **Key Stats Bar** — A row of quick stats (e.g., "6+ companies founded", "World's richest person", "150M+ Twitter followers").

5. **Famous Quote** — A standout quote from Musk to close the page.

## Design Style
- Clean, modern, minimal — dark theme with accent colors
- Uses existing shadcn components (Card, Badge, Separator)
- Smooth scroll, no navigation needed (single page)
- Fully responsive
